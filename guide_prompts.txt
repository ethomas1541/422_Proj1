SQ3R Survey: Before reading, skim the material for key concepts. Look at introduction, headings, cubheadings, figures, summaries, and review questions/practice problems.

SQ3R Question: Before reading the section, turn the heading into a question.

SQ3R Read: Keep the question in mind as you read and look for an answer to it.

SQ3R Recite: After each section, hide your notes and try to recall what you have read and try to answer your question.

SQ3R Review: When finished reading the chapter, look over your notes as a whole. Under each major section, hides the notes underneath and try to recite them.
